CyberCamera Privacy Policy / 赛博相机隐私政策

Last updated / 更新日期：July 14, 2026

Summary: CyberCamera lets users use camera, photo import, AI generation, and in-app purchases without registration. AI generation uses a third-party AI service only after explicit user consent.

摘要：赛博相机允许用户无需注册即可使用相机、相册导入、AI 生成和内购。AI 生成功能仅在用户明确同意后才会使用第三方 AI 服务。

1. Data We Collect / 我们收集的数据

Depending on how you use the app, CyberCamera may collect or process the following data:

  • Photos you actively capture or select for AI image generation.
  • Selected style, generated prompt, aspect ratio, quality, resolution, and other request parameters required for AI processing.
  • Account ID if you sign in, or a local random installation identifier if you use guest mode, for acceleration-credit balance, queue management, and purchase fulfillment.
  • Email address only if you choose to register or sign in.
  • Purchase history and transaction status for Apple in-app purchases.
  • If you choose to publish to the community feed: the album photo or generated image you actively publish, caption, selected director/professional settings, likes, comments, reports, block records, and share-reward counters needed to provide community features and moderation.
  • If you choose to submit feature suggestions or feedback: the title, detail, suggestion type, app version, and account ID or local random installation identifier needed to evaluate, develop, and show request status.
  • Basic service logs needed for security, troubleshooting, abuse prevention, and service reliability.

根据你的使用方式，赛博相机可能收集或处理以下数据：

  • 你主动拍摄或选择并用于 AI 图片生成的照片。
  • 所选风格、生成提示词、宽高比、质量、分辨率，以及 AI 处理所需的其他请求参数。
  • 如你登录，则使用账号 ID；如你以游客模式使用，则使用本机随机安装标识，用于加速次数余额、队列管理和内购发放。
  • 仅当你选择注册或登录时收集邮箱地址。
  • Apple App 内购的购买记录和交易状态。
  • 如你选择发布到作品流：用于公开展示、互动、一键复刻、分享奖励发放和内容安全管理的相册照片或生成图片、说明文字、所选导演或专业模式设置、点赞、评论、举报、屏蔽记录和分享奖励计数。
  • 如你选择提交功能建议或反馈：用于评估、开发和展示处理状态的标题、说明、建议类型、App 版本，以及账号 ID 或本机随机安装标识。
  • 用于安全、排障、防滥用和服务稳定性的基础服务日志。

2. How Data Is Collected / 数据如何收集

Photos and AI generation parameters are collected only when you actively choose a photo or take a photo and start AI generation. Account information is collected only when you choose to sign in. Purchase information is received through Apple's StoreKit and App Store server-side transaction flow.

照片和 AI 生成参数仅在你主动选择照片或拍照并发起 AI 生成时收集。账号信息仅在你选择登录时收集。购买信息通过 Apple StoreKit 和 App Store 服务端交易流程获取。

Community posts are collected only when you are signed in and actively publish an album photo or generated result to the community feed. Unpublished photos and generated results are not automatically made public.

作品流内容仅在你登录并主动发布相册照片或生成结果时收集。未发布的照片和生成结果不会自动公开。

Feature suggestions and feedback are collected only when you actively submit them in the app. Please do not include sensitive personal information such as phone numbers, addresses, or government IDs in feedback text.

功能建议和反馈仅在你于 App 内主动提交时收集。请勿在反馈文字中填写手机号、住址、身份证号等敏感个人信息。

3. Face Data / 面部数据

Face data collected: If a full photo actively captured or selected by the user contains a face, CyberCamera processes the visible facial image pixels contained in that photo. For transparency, this policy calls those visible pixels "face data." CyberCamera does not collect or create facial geometry, depth maps, facial landmarks, faceprints, face templates, vectors, embeddings, or other biometric identifiers, and does not use TrueDepth, ARKit, or facial-recognition technology.

收集的面部数据：如果用户主动拍摄或选择的整张照片中包含人脸，赛博相机会处理照片中可见的面部图像像素。为了透明起见，本政策将这些可见像素称为“面部数据”。赛博相机不收集或创建面部几何、深度图、面部关键点、面纹、人脸模板、向量、嵌入或其他生物识别标识，也不使用 TrueDepth、ARKit 或人脸识别技术。

Complete uses: Face data is used only to analyze the subject and scene in the submitted photo, preserve the person's recognizable appearance while applying the requested style, pose, expression, lighting, composition, and color treatment, generate and return the requested image, and troubleshoot that processing request. CyberCamera does not use face data to identify or verify a person, authenticate an account, match people across photos, track, profile, advertise, market, mine data, or train models.

完整用途：面部数据仅用于分析用户提交照片中的主体和场景，在改变用户选择的风格、姿势、表情、打光、构图和调色时保持人物的可识别外观，并生成、返回用户请求的图片以及排查该次处理错误。赛博相机不会将面部数据用于识别或验证个人身份、登录认证、跨图片匹配、追踪、画像、广告、营销、数据挖掘或模型训练。

Sharing and storage location: Only after explicit checkbox consent is the full source photo containing face data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS to CyberCamera's server hosted on Tencent Cloud in Singapore and then forwarded to the Lingya AI API processing environment. Lingya AI may use it only for the visual analysis and image generation requested by the user and must provide the same or equivalent protection. CyberCamera does not sell face data or share it with any other third party. Lingya AI receives no face template or biometric identifier because CyberCamera does not create one.

共享与存储位置：仅在用户明确勾选同意后，包含面部数据的整张源照片才会通过 HTTPS/TLS 发送至部署在新加坡 Tencent Cloud 的 CyberCamera 服务器，然后转发至 Lingya AI 的 API 处理环境。Lingya AI 仅可用于完成用户请求的视觉分析和图片生成，并需提供同等或更高标准保护。赛博相机不销售面部数据，也不会将其共享给其他第三方。因为赛博相机不创建人脸模板或生物识别标识，Lingya AI 也不会收到这类数据。

Retention: On CyberCamera's server, the source photo is stored only in a restricted generation-job record. It is removed from active job data immediately after generation completes or fails. For a delayed slow-queue task explicitly selected by the user, the source photo is retained only until processing and is automatically deleted within 7 days in all cases. Lingya AI may retain data transiently only as needed to complete the request and is required to delete it after completion, no later than 7 days. CyberCamera retains only the temporary result URL and non-image job status for up to 7 days.

保留期限：在 CyberCamera 服务器上，源照片只存储在受限制的生成任务记录中。生成完成或失败后，源照片会立即从活动任务数据中移除。对于用户主动选择的延时慢速队列，源照片仅保留至任务处理，且在任何情况下最迟 7 天自动删除。Lingya AI 只能在完成请求所必需的时间内暂存数据，并需在请求完成后删除，最长不超过 7 天。CyberCamera 仅保留临时结果 URL 和不包含图片的任务状态，最长 7 天。

Optional community publishing: If a user separately chooses to publish an album photo or generated result containing a face to the community feed, the entire image is stored as user-generated content on CyberCamera's Singapore server until the user deletes that post. Publishing is optional and separate from AI-generation consent. Deleting the post removes the image from public display and active storage.

可选作品发布：如果用户另行主动将包含人脸的相册照片或生成结果发布到作品流，整张图片会作为用户生成内容存储于 CyberCamera 的新加坡服务器，直至用户删除该作品。发布是可选操作，与 AI 生成授权分开。删除作品时，图片会从公开展示和活动存储中移除。

User control: If the user does not consent, no source photo or face data is sent for AI generation. The user can stop future collection by not starting another generation, delete a community post in the app, or request deletion by emailing minecyber@126.com. We respond to deletion requests within 15 business days.

用户控制：如果用户不同意，不会发送任何源照片或面部数据用于 AI 生成。用户可通过不再发起生成来停止后续收集，可在 App 内删除作品，也可发送邮件至 minecyber@126.com 申请删除。我们会在 15 个工作日内回复删除请求。

4. Third-Party AI Data Sharing / 第三方 AI 数据共享

Before the first AI generation request for the current disclosure version, CyberCamera displays an AI data permission screen in the app and requires explicit checkbox consent. If you do not consent, CyberCamera will not send your photo, prompt, request parameters, account ID, or local identifier for AI generation.

在当前披露版本下首次发起 AI 生成请求前，赛博相机会在 App 内显示 AI 数据授权页面，并要求你明确勾选同意。如果你不同意，赛博相机不会发送你的照片、提示词、请求参数、账号 ID 或本机标识用于 AI 生成。

With your consent, the following data is sent to CyberCamera servers and then forwarded to Lingya AI, a third-party AI service provider: the selected or captured photo, selected style, generated prompt, required request parameters, and account ID or local random installation identifier.

经你同意后，以下数据会发送至 CyberCamera 服务器，并转发给第三方 AI 服务提供商 Lingya AI：你选择或拍摄的照片、所选风格、生成提示词、必要请求参数，以及账号 ID 或本机随机安装标识。

The data is used only for visual analysis, AI image generation, result delivery, acceleration-credit balance, queue management, purchase fulfillment, feature evaluation, security, and troubleshooting. CyberCamera confirms that Lingya AI is required to provide the same or equal protection for shared data and may use the shared data only to complete the AI processing requested by the user. CyberCamera does not use third-party advertising and does not track users across apps or websites.

这些数据仅用于视觉分析、AI 图片生成、返回生成结果、加速次数余额、队列管理、内购发放、功能评估、安全和错误排查。赛博相机确认 Lingya AI 需对共享数据提供相同或同等程度保护，并且仅可将共享数据用于完成用户请求的 AI 处理。赛博相机不使用第三方广告，也不会跨 App 或网站追踪用户。

5. User Choice / 用户选择

You can use camera, photo import, AI generation, and in-app purchases without registration. Registration is optional and is used for account management and cross-device acceleration-credit sync. If the shared data types, purposes, or third-party AI provider change, CyberCamera will request consent again before sending data for AI generation.

你无需注册即可使用相机、相册导入、AI 生成和内购。注册是可选的，仅用于账号管理和跨设备加速次数同步。如果共享数据类型、用途或第三方 AI 服务提供商发生变化，赛博相机会在再次发送数据用于 AI 生成前重新请求你的授权。

6. Community Safety / 社区安全

For community features, CyberCamera provides text filtering, user reporting, user blocking, and support contact. Reported or policy-violating content may be hidden, removed, or restricted. You may delete your own community posts in the app. Signed-in users may receive 1 free generation credit for each of their first 5 successful public community posts, subject to safety and anti-abuse limits.

对于作品流等社区功能，赛博相机提供文本过滤、用户举报、屏蔽用户和支持联系入口。被举报或违反规则的内容可能被隐藏、删除或限制展示。你可以在 App 内删除自己的公开作品。登录用户前 5 次成功发布公开作品可各获得 1 次免费生成次数，奖励受内容安全和防滥用规则约束。

7. Contact / 联系我们

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at minecyber@126.com.

如果你对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过 minecyber@126.com 联系我们。
